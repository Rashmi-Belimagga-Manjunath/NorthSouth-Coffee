# NorthSouth Coffee Loyalty Recovery System

## Executive Summary

NorthSouth Coffee operates 120 locations and has experienced a 40% decline in loyalty programme engagement over the past six months.

Customers are downloading the loyalty app, claiming the initial free drink reward, and failing to engage thereafter.

To diagnose and solve this issue, a Five-Agent AI Workflow was developed.

---

## Business Problem

Current Challenges:

- High signup rates
- Low repeat engagement
- Weak customer retention
- Limited personalization
- Reward fatigue

---

## Agent Architecture

## Findings

The loyalty programme suffers from:

1. One-time reward behavior
2. Poor habit formation
3. Lack of customer segmentation
4. Generic messaging
5. Low perceived reward value
