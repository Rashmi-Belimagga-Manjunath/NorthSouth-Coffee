☕ NorthSouth Coffee Loyalty Recovery System

Five-Agent AI Workflow for Diagnosing and Solving Loyalty Churn

Executive Summary

NorthSouth Coffee operates 120 locations and has experienced a 40% decline in loyalty programme engagement over the last six months. Customers sign up, redeem a free drink, and stop engaging with the app.


A Five-Agent AI Workflow was designed to diagnose root causes, redesign the loyalty experience, create an implementation roadmap, and develop retention campaigns.

Business Problem

  • 40% drop in loyalty engagement
  • High signup rates but low retention
  • Weak customer habit formation
  • Poor personalization
  • Limited customer segmentation

Root Cause Analysis

  • Customers are motivated only by the free signup reward.
  • No meaningful incentive for a second visit.
  • Generic loyalty communications.
  • No customer segmentation.
  • Rewards do not create long-term habits.
