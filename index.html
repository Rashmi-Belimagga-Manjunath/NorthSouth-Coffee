Executive Summary

NorthSouth Coffee experienced a 40% drop in loyalty engagement over six months. Customers signed up for the loyalty app, redeemed a free drink, and rarely returned. A five-agent AI workflow was created to diagnose root causes, redesign the loyalty experience, build an implementation plan, and create retention campaigns.

Executive Report

Root Cause Analysis

  • Customers only engage for the free signup reward.
  • No meaningful second-visit incentive.
  • Weak habit-building mechanisms.
  • Generic communications.
  • No personalized customer journeys.
  • Limited segmentation and targeting.

Recommended Solution

  • Free drink on signup.
  • Double points on second visit.
  • Free bakery item on third visit.
  • VIP tier at fifth visit.
  • Customer-specific rewards.
  • Automated retention campaigns.
